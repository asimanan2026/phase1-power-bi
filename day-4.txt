Day 4 – Reflection (Data Shaping)

1. What is the difference between cleaning and shaping data?
   Data cleaning focuses on fixing errors such as missing values, duplicates, and incorrect data types. Data shaping focuses on transforming data into a suitable structure for analysis, such as splitting columns, filtering rows, and creating new calculated fields.

2. What transformation did you find most useful?
   I found splitting columns and adding a custom column to be most useful, as they help in better understanding and analyzing the data.

3. Check-in: Walk through one transformation you did and why

Transformation: Split Date Column

Before:
Date = "2024-01-05"

After:
Year = 2024
Month = 01
Day = 05

Reason:
This makes it easier to analyze data by month or year and helps in creating time-based visualizations like trends.
