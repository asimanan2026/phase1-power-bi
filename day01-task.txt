---------------------------------------------------
5 EXAMPLES OF DATA IN MY DAILY LIFE
---------------------------------------------------

1. PHONE SCREEN TIME
   - What data: Daily and weekly screen time in hours, broken down by app
   - Who collects it: My phone's operating system (Android/iOS Screen Time feature)
   - Decision it helps make: Decide which apps are wasting my time and set daily
     limits to improve focus and productivity

2. BUS / AUTO TIMINGS (LOCAL TRANSPORT IN KOZHIKODE)
   - What data: Arrival and departure times of KSRTC buses and auto-rickshaws,
     route frequencies, peak-hour delays
   - Who collects it: KSRTC (Kerala State Road Transport Corporation),
     and partly through Google Maps real-time data from commuters
   - Decision it helps make: Choose the fastest route to college or work,
     and decide whether to leave early or wait for a less crowded bus

3. MARKS AND EXAM SCORES
   - What data: Internal assessment marks, semester exam scores, attendance percentage
   - Who collects it: My college/school administration and teachers
   - Decision it helps make: Identify subjects where I need to study more,
     predict final grades, decide which topics to focus on before exams

4. RAINFALL DATA IN KOZHIKODE
   - What data: Daily rainfall in mm, monthly totals, flood alerts
   - Who collects it: India Meteorological Department (IMD) and Kerala State
     Disaster Management Authority
   - Decision it helps make: Decide whether to carry an umbrella, whether
     schools/colleges will be closed, and whether travel is safe during
     monsoon season

5. UPI / DIGITAL PAYMENT HISTORY
   - What data: Amount spent, merchant name, date and time, category of spend
     (food, transport, shopping, etc.)
   - Who collects it: My bank and UPI apps like Google Pay or PhonePe
   - Decision it helps make: Track where my money is going each month,
     identify unnecessary expenses, and plan a personal budget

---------------------------------------------------
MY ONE BIG DATA QUESTION
---------------------------------------------------

"Does rainfall in Kozhikode affect student attendance and exam performance —
and if so, which months should schools plan extra classes or online backup
sessions to make up for weather-related disruptions?"
